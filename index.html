About us

Unique Experience in the Real Estate Industry

CAV Investment Group was founded by Simon Baker, Serial Investor in and Advisor to the Global PropTech Industry.

Former CEO and Managing Director of the REA Group, during his eight years leadership, REA Group’s revenues increased from AUD$4m to AUD$155m and an AUD$6m loss was turned into an EBITDA profit of AUD$30m with the market cap increasing from AUD$8m to a high of AUD$1 billion.

With more than 30 years of experience in the classifieds and marketplaces industry, Simon has also founded more than 10 different companies and has been the Chairman of several ASX listed companies such as iProperty Group, CarAdvice and Mitula Group amongst others.

Today Simon is the Chairman of the ASX listed Proptech Group Ltd. (ASX:TPG).

Contact us to find unparallel expertise in Property Classifieds, Online Brokers, Home Search, iBuyers and Marketplaces in general. We are looking for top-notch Founders of Next-generation companies in the Global Real Estate and Proptech arena.
